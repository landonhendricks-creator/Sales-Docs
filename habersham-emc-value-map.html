Stage 1 → 2
Business Objectives
  • Modernize aging Mitel MiVoice Business 3300 infrastructure (~20 yrs old) with a cloud-native UCaaS + CCaaS platform
  • Eliminate reporting discrepancies between Power BI and Mitel that are causing board-level frustration
  • Ensure business continuity and communication resilience during storm / mass-outage events
  • Improve member experience through post-call surveys, omnichannel contact, and measurable QA
  • Maintain and preserve existing Creovai QA/sentiment workflow without disruption to Valerie's team
  • Complete vendor evaluation and selection by mid-to-late June 2026 ahead of August budget cycle
Challenges & Pain Points
  • Power BI + Mitel data mismatch — inaccurate call reporting presented to board; undermines credibility of IT ops data
  • CT Solutions (Mitel support partner) is gone — no support coverage on aging PBX; Tony managing hardware risk alone
  • Ice storm event (70% outage, 20-hr shifts) exposed serious gaps in IVR flexibility and outbound communication at scale
  • No dynamic IVR storm mode — agents manually re-routing calls during crisis; no pre-built outage messaging
  • Recording retention compliance — capital credit disputes require recordings up to 3–4 years old; current solution unclear on 7-yr policy
  • Only ~half of employees have DIDs today — limits direct member communication and accountability
  • Chat currently handled via Mitel Ignite — separate platform, fragmented omnichannel experience
Strategies
  • Replace Mitel MiVoice Business 3300 with 8x8 X Series — unified UCaaS + CCaaS on a single cloud platform
  • Integrate 8x8 with Creovai via FTP/API to preserve existing QA and sentiment analysis workflows
  • Deploy 8x8 AI Studio for outage/storm IVR mode — pre-built menu activated during mass events
  • Leverage 8x8 native reporting + Power BI connector to eliminate data mismatch at the board level
  • Reuse existing Mitel 6940/6930 SIP phones to reduce hardware cost during transition
  • Enable SSO via Azure AD / SCIM for seamless provisioning aligned to existing identity management

  • Second demo (expanded stakeholders + executive) targeting beginning of May 2026

  • Accurate, real-time call data feeding Power BI — board-ready reporting with zero reconciliation overhead
