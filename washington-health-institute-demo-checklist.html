  • If Max asks about API access or customization — confirm 8x8 open APIs, but re-anchor on no-code first (no devs at WHI)
  • If Max raises existing phone system compatibility — confirm WHI is already on 8x8 UCaaS (X1/X4), this is an add-on

⚡ Objection Handling — Quick Reference

  • "We're already on 8x8 — why do we need to pay more?" Response: "Your existing X1/X4 licenses give you great UCaaS. CC voice (X6) adds the inbound patient queue, routing, analytics, and supervisor tools — it's a separate capability layer. And AI Studio is consumption-based — you only pay for what the bot handles."
  • "Can't we just use ECW's built-in phone features?" Response: "ECW's phone is designed for scheduling — not contact center management. It can't give you real-time queue analytics, supervisor wallboards, skills-based routing, or AI voice bots. 8x8 sits alongside ECW and makes it smarter."
  • "How long does this take to set up?" Response: "A typical CC deployment runs 4–6 weeks. EMR integration adds time depending on the connector path — that's why we want to nail down the ECW path before we scope. No surprises."
  • "What if the AI bot makes a mistake with a patient?" Response: "The bot handles structured tasks — collect name, confirm med, route or take message. It never makes clinical decisions. Any complex situation escalates to a live agent immediately. You control every path."
  • "We don't have IT staff to manage this." Response: "That's exactly why customers like WHI choose 8x8. We manage the infrastructure. Your team uses a web portal — no servers, no IT tickets. And Compass 360 means we actively tune the bot for you monthly."
