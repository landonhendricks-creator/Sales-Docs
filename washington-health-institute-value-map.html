Key Pain Points
  • 📵
    Calls going unanswered / high abandonment rate — no queue visibility, no overflow routing. Staff stretched across 3 sites.
  • 📊
    UC analytics are insufficient — Michael said directly: "They stink." No KPIs on patient-facing call performance, no queue depth insight.
  • 💊
    Manual prescription refill workflow — voicemail box checked every 2 hours. No real-time visibility. Staff must proactively check, creating gaps.
  • 🔗
    No EMR screen pop — receptionists answer calls blind. Patient context (name, DOB, appointments, location) must be looked up manually in ECW.
  • 🌍

  • 💸
    Cost pressure — operating costs increasing while medical reimbursement declining. ROI and cost containment are required.

AI Studio — Identified Use Cases for WHI
Phase 1 — No Integration Required
  • 📻
    Auto-attendant / call routing — front-end every inbound call, route to correct department/queue. Immediate abandonment reduction.
  • ❓
    FAQ handling — clinic hours, directions, parking, accepted insurance. Pulls from knowledge base. Zero integration needed.
  • 🌍
    Language auto-detection — patient speaks, bot identifies language and responds accordingly. No menu required.
Phase 2 — With ECW Integration
  • ✅
    Patient verification — AI verifies caller identity (name, DOB) before transferring to human or taking action. HIPAA-compliant flow.
  • 📅
    Appointment scheduling / rescheduling — reads availability across all 3 clinic locations via ECW integration. Confirms or reschedules in 1–2 min conversation.
  • 📍
    Location-aware routing — identifies which of the 3 WHI clinics the patient is associated with and routes accordingly.
Phase 2 — Clinical Workflow
  • 💊
    Prescription refill routing — AI identifies refill intent, verifies patient, routes to refill queue (Engage mobile for nurse). Eliminates voicemail-every-2-hours workflow.
  • 🔁
    Escalation to human — always available. If language unclear, complex issue, or patient requests, AI hands off to live agent with context summary.
  • 💰
    Estimated cost: ~$1,000–$1,500/month in AI credits based on ~150 calls/day. (~8 credits/min, ~$0.03/min equivalent)
ECW Integration — Critical Decision Point
  • 🔍
    EMR: eClinicalWorks (ECW) — existing system, not changing
  • 🛠️
    Option A — 8x8 Native Integration (preferred): Landon believes 8x8 may have built ECW integration directly. Lower cost. Must confirm with pro services before demo. Verify
  • 🤝
    Option B — SpinSci (s-p-i-n-s-c-i): Most-used EMR integration middleware for CC vendors. Has ECW integration confirmed. "Buy once, cry once" — setup can be costly. 3rd Party
  • ✨
    Either way, integration pops into the 8x8 agent desktop — NOT into ECW. One pane of glass for agent: patient name, DOB, appointments, location, all 3 clinics.
  • ⚠️
    Michael & Max asked specifically: "Would this be an external tool or pop into ECW?" Answer: pops into 8x8. They were satisfied with this.
  • 📋
    SpinSci also benefits human agents (not just the AI bot) — screen pop for every inbound call, not just AI-handled ones.
